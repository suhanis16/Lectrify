Who are we?

Just a bunch of college students trying to make learning fun.

What are we doing?

At our platform, we are revolutionizing learning through the Protege Effect. Experience interactive teaching by guiding an AI student and bridging knowledge gaps. Engage in meaningful learning interactions, receive feedback, and strengthen your understanding.

Why are we doing this?

Studies show that students retain only 10-20% of the information they learn through lectures. And this is exactly what we're trying to fix through our website. We are transforming education by empowering users to actively participate in the learning process and unlock their teaching potential.
